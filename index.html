Take yourself on an adventure and find three hidden bones by moving Roscoe with your nose. Begin the game by clapping!

Bees!

Find the bone hidden in the field and leave before more bees attack

Water!

Find the bone in the water and dry yourself off by leaving the pond

Danger!

Find the bone inside the house and run away from the enemy before they bite!

Play Again!

Put your hands together to play again!
